FAQ

Frequently asked
questions

Short answers to the questions we get every week. If yours isn't here, we'll cover it on the intro call — no slide deck, just answers.
1
What do I get when I hire you?
A production-ready website hosted on Vercel or GitHub, a Notion workspace wired to your forms, and any n8n automations we scoped — all on your own accounts so you own the keys.
2
How does the n8n automation work?
We run n8n flows that watch your inbox and forms, draft replies with an LLM trained on your past responses, generate quotes from a pricing sheet, and push everything into Notion or your CRM. You approve, the customer hears back in minutes.
3
Can you integrate with my existing CRM?
Yes. We've shipped flows for HubSpot, Pipedrive, Attio, Airtable, and plain Google Sheets. If it has an API or a webhook, we can wire it up.
4
What does a typical project cost and take?
Most landings ship in 5–10 days. Sites with custom forms, Notion dashboards, and one or two automations run 7–14 days. We quote a fixed price after a 30-minute scoping call.
5
What happens after launch?
You keep the GitHub repo, the Vercel project, and the n8n instance. We offer a monthly retainer for tweaks and new flows, but there's no lock-in — keep us on call or take it from here.
